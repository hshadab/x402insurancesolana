1. Agents Buy Insurance
Agents pay a premium (1% of coverage amount) via x402 protocol to insure against merchant fraud. Payments are verified on-chain.

2. NovaNet Generates Proofs
When agent submits fraud claim, NovaNet generates a zero-knowledge proof that verifies whether fraud took place, without revealing private merchant data.

3. Automatic USDC Refunds
If the proof verifies, USDC refunds are automatically issued on Base Mainnet. All proofs are publicly auditable.
